About MoM

Mom’s family is one of pioneer group who settle down for farming in Khao-Khai village, Chumphon in 1970s.

Mom started farming like the old way since 2005. The natural farming is consiting of growing trees and mixed economic crops all together. This type farming allows MoM to keep her farm like“forestry” while generated good income all years around. It is also help increase biodiversity, reduce land erosion.

Let be part of MoM Co-farming and help MoM family farming more natural way. It is Mom mission to make sure that all her family members have dept free farming and their all restore farmland back to be more sustainable future.
