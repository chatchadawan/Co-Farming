The History of our Farm

In 2005, Mom started farming like the old way of natural farming by grow wooden trees and mixed economic crops all together. This type farming allows MoM to keep her farm like“forestry” while generated good income from various type of corps all years around. It is also help increase biodiversity, reduce land erosion.
